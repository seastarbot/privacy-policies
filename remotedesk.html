Privacy Policy

RemoteDesk · Effective Date: March 31, 2026

1. Introduction

RemoteDesk ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

RemoteDesk is a remote desktop application that allows you to access and control your computer from your iOS device.

🔐 Security Notice: RemoteDesk uses encrypted connections for all remote sessions. We do not have access to your remote desktop sessions or the data transmitted during connections.

2. Information We Collect

Information stored locally:

  • Connection profiles — Server addresses, usernames, and connection settings you enter are stored locally on your device. Passwords are stored in the iOS Keychain.
  • App preferences — Display settings, keyboard shortcuts, and gesture configurations are stored locally.

Network data:

  • Remote connections — The App establishes direct connections to the remote computers you specify. Screen data, keyboard input, and mouse events are transmitted between your device and the remote computer.

We do not collect, log, or have access to any data transmitted during your remote sessions.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To establish and maintain remote desktop connections
  • To store your connection profiles for quick access
  • To remember your display and input preferences

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store — For app distribution and in-app purchases. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

RemoteDesk establishes direct connections between your device and your remote computers. We do not use relay servers, and we do not have visibility into your connection data.

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Connection passwords are stored in the iOS Keychain, which provides hardware-level encryption
  • All remote session data is transmitted over encrypted protocols (SSH/TLS as configured by the user)
  • We operate no relay servers — connections are peer-to-peer or direct to your configured hosts

6. Network Permissions

RemoteDesk requires network access to connect to remote computers. The App may request:

  • Local Network — To discover and connect to computers on your local network
  • Network access — To connect to remote computers over the internet

7. Children's Privacy

RemoteDesk is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not collect personal information from any users, including children under 13.

8. Your Rights

  • Delete connection profiles at any time within the App
  • Clear stored passwords through the App or iOS Settings
  • Uninstall the App to remove all local data (Keychain data may persist until explicitly cleared)

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
