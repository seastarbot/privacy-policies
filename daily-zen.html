Privacy Policy

Daily Zen · Effective Date: March 31, 2026

1. Introduction

Daily Zen ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Daily Zen is a meditation and mindfulness application that provides daily guided meditation sessions, breathing exercises, and mindfulness reminders.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information:

  • Meditation history — Your session history, streaks, and total meditation time are stored locally on your device.
  • Notification preferences — Your reminder settings are stored locally to send meditation reminders.
  • HealthKit (optional) — If you grant permission, we may write mindful minutes to Apple Health to track your meditation practice. We only write data; we do not read any other Health data.

We do not collect your name, email, location, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us for support.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To track your meditation progress and streaks
  • To send meditation reminders at your chosen times
  • To optionally sync meditation data with Apple Health

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store — For app distribution and in-app purchases. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple HealthKit — Only if you opt in. Data is stored on your device and governed by Apple's privacy policies.
  • Apple Push Notifications — To deliver meditation reminders. Notification permissions are managed through iOS settings.

We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics tracking, or any third-party data collection services.

5. Data Storage & Security

All meditation data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit any data to external servers. HealthKit data, if enabled, is managed entirely by Apple's Health framework on your device.

6. In-App Purchases

Daily Zen may offer premium meditation content through Apple's In-App Purchase system. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

7. Children's Privacy

Daily Zen is suitable for all ages. We do not collect personal information from any users, including children under 13.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can reset your meditation history within the App or remove all data by uninstalling the App.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
