Privacy Policy

WriteFlow · Effective Date: March 31, 2026

1. Introduction

WriteFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

WriteFlow is an AI-powered writing assistant that helps you improve your writing through suggestions, grammar corrections, and style enhancements.

⚠️ AI Processing Notice: WriteFlow uses AI services to process your text for writing suggestions. Your text is sent to AI providers for processing and is not stored by them beyond the time needed to generate a response.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Text content — Text you input for AI processing (grammar checks, rewrites, style suggestions). This is sent to AI service providers for real-time processing.
  • Writing preferences — Your preferred writing style, tone settings, and custom dictionary are stored locally on your device.
  • Document history — Your saved documents and drafts are stored locally on your device.

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage statistics — Anonymized data about feature usage (e.g., how often grammar check is used) to improve the App.
  • Device information — Device type and app version for optimization.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered writing suggestions by sending your text to AI processing services
  • To save your documents and preferences locally
  • To improve app features through anonymized usage data

We do not use your text content for AI model training.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store — For app distribution and in-app purchases. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • AI Service Providers — Your text is sent to AI providers (such as OpenAI or similar) for real-time processing. These providers process your text to generate writing suggestions and do not store your content beyond the processing period. Please refer to the respective provider's privacy policy for details.
  • Cloud sync (optional) — If you enable cloud sync, documents are encrypted and synced via iCloud.

We do not use advertising SDKs or third-party tracking services.

5. Data Storage & Security

Documents and preferences are stored locally on your device. Text sent for AI processing is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS). We do not store your text content on our servers. AI providers process text in real-time and do not retain it.

6. Children's Privacy

WriteFlow is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

  • Delete individual documents or all data within the App
  • Use the App offline (AI features require internet, but local editing works offline)
  • Uninstall the App to remove all local data
  • Contact us to request information about data processed by AI providers

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
