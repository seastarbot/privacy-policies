Privacy Policy

SystemPulse · Effective Date: March 31, 2026

✅ 100% On-Device. SystemPulse reads system metrics locally and never transmits any data externally.

1. Introduction

SystemPulse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

SystemPulse is a system monitoring application that displays device performance metrics such as battery status, storage usage, memory, and network information.

2. Information We Collect

SystemPulse reads system metrics from your device. None of this data leaves your device.

The App accesses the following device information:

  • Battery status — Charge level, charging state, and battery health
  • Storage information — Total and available storage space
  • Memory usage — RAM usage and availability
  • Network information — Wi-Fi status, cellular data usage (from iOS APIs)
  • Device information — Device model, iOS version, and system uptime

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of this information. It is read in real-time and displayed within the App only.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store — For app distribution. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not use any analytics, advertising, tracking services, or third-party SDKs of any kind.

4. Data Storage & Security

SystemPulse does not store any data persistently. System metrics are read in real-time and displayed temporarily in memory. No data is saved to disk or transmitted anywhere.

5. Permissions

The App may request the following iOS permissions:

  • Local Network — To display local network information (optional)

All permissions are optional and can be denied without affecting core functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

SystemPulse is suitable for all ages. We do not collect personal information from any users, including children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Since SystemPulse does not collect or store any personal data, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
