Privacy Policy

Agent Guide · Effective Date: March 31, 2026

1. Introduction

Agent Guide ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Agent Guide is an educational application that provides tutorials, guides, and resources about AI Agents.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Bookmarks & Progress — Your reading progress and bookmarked articles are stored locally on your device.
  • Optional Account — If you choose to create an account to sync your progress across devices, we collect your email address and an encrypted password.

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data — We may collect anonymized data about which tutorials are viewed and completed to improve content quality.
  • Device information — Device type, iOS version, and app version for troubleshooting and optimization.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the tutorial content and app experience
  • To sync your learning progress across devices (if you create an account)
  • To analyze aggregated usage patterns to improve our content
  • To respond to your support requests

4. Third-Party Services

Agent Guide may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store — For app distribution. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloud hosting provider — To host tutorial content and optional account data. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • Analytics — We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how content is consumed. No personal data is shared with analytics providers.

We do not sell your data to third parties or use third-party advertising networks.

5. Data Storage & Security

Local data is stored securely on your device. Account data (if applicable) is encrypted and stored on secure cloud servers. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS) and at rest.

6. Children's Privacy

Agent Guide is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

  • Access — You can request a copy of your data by contacting us.
  • Deletion — You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
  • Local data — Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
