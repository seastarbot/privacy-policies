Privacy Policy

Streak · Effective Date: March 31, 2026

1. Introduction

Streak ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Streak is a habit tracking application that helps you build and maintain daily habits through streak counting and reminders.

2. Information We Collect

Streak is designed with privacy in mind. All data stays on your device.

  • Habit data — Your habits, check-in history, streaks, and notes are stored locally on your device.
  • Notification preferences — Reminder times and settings are stored locally.
  • Widgets — Home screen widgets read habit data from local storage only.

We do not collect any personal information, account data, or analytics. We do not have servers that receive any of your data.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely within the App to:

  • Display your habits and streak progress
  • Send habit reminders at your chosen times
  • Show habit data on home screen widgets

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store — For app distribution and in-app purchases. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • iCloud (optional) — If you enable iCloud sync, your habit data is synced across your Apple devices via Apple's iCloud service. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.
  • Apple Push Notifications — For habit reminders, processed entirely by Apple.

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms. If iCloud sync is enabled, data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account. We operate no servers and have no access to your data.

6. Data Export

You can export your habit data at any time through the App's settings in CSV or JSON format.

7. Children's Privacy

Streak is suitable for all ages. We do not collect personal information from any users, including children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual habits or all data within the App
  • Export your data at any time
  • Disable iCloud sync to keep data only on your device
  • Uninstall the App to remove all local data

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
