Privacy Policy

Calm Sound · Effective Date: March 31, 2026

1. Introduction

Calm Sound ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Calm Sound is a white noise and sleep aid application that plays ambient sounds to help you relax and sleep better.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Calm Sound operates entirely on your device. Specifically:

  • No account registration — You do not need to create an account to use the App.
  • No personal data collection — We do not collect your name, email, location, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • Sound preferences — Your sound mix preferences and timer settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us.

3. Third-Party Services

Calm Sound may use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Apple App Store — For app distribution and in-app purchases. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Analytics — If you opt in to sharing analytics with developers through iOS settings, Apple may provide us with anonymized, aggregated crash and usage data.

We do not use any third-party advertising SDKs or tracking services.

4. Data Storage & Security

All app data (sound preferences, sleep timer settings, favorites) is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. No data is transmitted to external servers.

5. In-App Purchases

Calm Sound may offer premium sound packs through Apple's In-App Purchase system. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

6. Children's Privacy

Calm Sound does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages and does not contain any content inappropriate for children.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete. All data remains on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
